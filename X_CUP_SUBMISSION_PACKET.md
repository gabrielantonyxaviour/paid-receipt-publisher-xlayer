# X Cup Submission Packet

## Project

Project name: X Cup Proof Receipts

One-line description: Signed, hash-chained World Cup prediction, hedge, denial, refund, and settlement receipts that can be anchored on X Layer.

Project highlights:

- Converts every World Cup fan-agent action into a signed receipt: prediction stake, hedge route, denied risky bet, void-market refund, and settlement proof.
- Produces a verifiable hash chain with ed25519 signatures and a final chain root.
- Includes an X Layer `ReceiptRegistry` contract and live RPC read evidence for testnet and mainnet.
- Keeps unfinished actions honest: live writes are blocked until a funded deployer or approved wallet path exists.

## X Cup Alignment

Track fit: World Cup prediction markets, trading, social proof, and AI Agent infrastructure.

Why it fits: X Cup projects need World Cup-native UX, X Layer deployment surface, completion evidence, and on-chain verifiability. This project gives World Cup prediction/trading agents a receipt layer judges can inspect and users can later anchor on X Layer.
